Welcome to IllegalAnimals

We are glad you are here, but who are we?


IllegalAnimals is a company founded on the idea of preserving exotic animals by ensuring we have have a supply of customers to purchase those animals. In other words, we legally obtain illegal, exotic animals and sell them to you!

We would love for you to be one of our loyal customers. We have multiple species to choose from different animal kingdoms.

Choose from Reptiles, Mammals, Avians, and Amphibians!

If you have have any questions refer to our FAQ. If your question isn't answered from our FAQ, don't hesitate reach out and contact us.

We are currently based in San Francisco and have lots of experience. We have been working together for over 2 weeks!
